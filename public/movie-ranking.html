The Best Movies According to Rajdip

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Titanic

This movie brings a tear to your eyes. If you want to cry and melt down to a glamurous love story, I strongly recommend that you watch this movie. Titanic is well worth seeing.

Avatar

Avatar brings forward the true nature of how humans have chosen to treat the land.

Life of Pi

Life of Pi is an exhilarating drama about the mysteries which light up our lives and have no rhyme or reason on their own; the faith that enables us to make a leap into the dark; the teachings of animals; the fierce and tranquil sides of nature; and the powerful instinct we all have for survival.
